ABOUT

CBUKAS CONTRACTORS LIMITED is a trusted provider of high-quality building materials and professional contracting services. We supply aluminium roofing sheets, stone-coated tiles, doors, POP ceiling materials, gutters, nails, and sealants to contractors, resellers, and homeowners in Calabar, Cross River State, and nationwide. Our team also offers expert installation, maintenance, and technical support to ensure every project is completed with precision, durability, and excellence.
