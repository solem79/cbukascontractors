About Us

About CBUKAS CONTRACTORS LIMITED

At CBUKAS CONTRACTORS LIMITED, we provide professional general building contracting, engineering services, and the sale, supply, and installation of quality building materials for residential, commercial, and industrial projects across Nigeria.

Our operations cover roofing systems, stone-coated tiles, aluminium and metal roofing sheets, POP ceiling installations, and complete building support services. From contract execution to engineering supervision and material supply, we deliver durable, functional, and cost-effective construction solutions tailored to our clients’ needs.

Our Story

From a modest start to becoming a trusted name in general building contracting, engineering services, and quality building material supply, CBUKAS CONTRACTORS LIMITED has remained committed to professionalism, durability, and client satisfaction.

2016

CBUKAS CONTRACTORS LIMITED was established with a focus on general building contracting, engineering works, and reliable supply of essential building materials.

2018

Expanded operations into roofing systems, POP ceiling installations, and professional material installation services for residential and commercial projects.

2021

Successfully executed and supported numerous construction projects across multiple locations, strengthening our reputation for quality workmanship and dependable service delivery.

2025

Recognized as a dependable contractor and building materials supplier, trusted for engineering expertise, durable materials, and timely project execution.

ABOUT

CBUKAS CONTRACTORS LIMITED is a trusted provider of high-quality building materials and professional contracting services.
