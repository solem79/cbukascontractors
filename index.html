About CBUKAS CONTRACTORS LIMITED

General Building Contractors, Engineering & Material Supply

CBUKAS CONTRACTORS LIMITED is a CAC-registered general contracting and engineering firm delivering projects across Nigeria. We handle building contracts, engineering works, and the sale, supply, and installation of high-quality building materials for residential, commercial, and industrial projects.


Our expertise covers roofing materials supply and installation, stone-coated tiles, aluminium and metal roofing sheets, POP ceiling works, and other essential building solutions. With a strong commitment to quality, durability, and professional execution, we manage projects from material supply to complete installation — delivering reliable results that stand the test of time.
