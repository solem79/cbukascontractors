Our Services

CBUKAS Contractors Limited provides professional contracting, high-quality roofing materials, POP ceiling solutions, and a complete range of building materials for residential, commercial, and industrial projects in Nigeria.

General Building Contracting

We manage building projects from start to finish — including planning, construction, project management, and quality assurance — ensuring your building is delivered on time and to specification.

Engineering & Installation Services

Our team provides professional installation services for roofing sheets, POP ceilings, gutters, doors, windows, and other building components, combining technical expertise with precision execution.

Building Materials Supply

We supply premium-quality building materials including aluminium roofing sheets, stone-coated tiles, POP boards, fasteners, gutters, and more — sourced from trusted manufacturers for durability and reliability.

Roofing Installation & Repairs

From residential homes to commercial buildings, our team installs and repairs aluminium sheets, long-span roofing, and stone-coated tiles to provide weather-resistant, long-lasting roofs.

POP & Ceiling Solutions

We offer design, supply, and installation of POP ceilings, ensuring aesthetic appeal, functionality, and durability for interiors of residential and commercial projects.

Contractor & Reseller Partnerships

CBUKAS Contractors partners with contractors, property developers, and resellers, providing bulk supply, flexible pricing, and expert material advice for efficient project completion.
